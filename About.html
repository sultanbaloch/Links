About The Organization
NAVTTC is an apex body at national level to regulate, facilitate and provide policy direction in Vocational &
Technical Training. The Commission is establishing and promoting linkages among various stakeholders existing
at national as well as international level. Since its inception, NAVTTC has given a high priority to un-addressed
areas and challenges being faced by TVET.
